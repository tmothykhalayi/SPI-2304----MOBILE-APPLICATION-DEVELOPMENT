Heading 1: The Main Title

This is the main heading of the page. The <h1> tag is typically used for the most important heading on a webpage. It usually represents the primary topic or the title of a page.

Heading 2: A Subheading

The <h2> tag is used for subheadings that are important but secondary to the <h1>. You can use it to break your content into more digestible sections.

Heading 3: A Smaller Subheading

The <h3> tag is typically used for even smaller subsections within the content. It can be helpful for organizing your page into logical blocks for better readability.
